What's a Story game?

A story game is a word game where you type in words that will be substituted for blanks scattered throughout a story. You will be promoted for words such as:

Noun - a noun is a person, place, or thing.

Verb - verbs are action words such as Jogged,sing, or fly.

Adjective - an adjective is a word that describe a noun. an adjective can say a nouns shape,size,color,or age along with many other descriptors.

Adverb - an adverb modifies a verb to end in -ly such as quietly,softly, or hastily.
